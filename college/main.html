About us

St. Francis PU College stands as a beacon of academic excellence and holistic development, providing a nurturing environment for pre-university education. Located in the heart of our city, the college is renowned for its dedicated faculty, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and a diverse range of programs that cater to the academic and extracurricular aspirations of its students. At St. Francis PU College, the curriculum is designed to not only impart in-depth knowledge but also to foster critical thinking and creativity. The college offers an array of courses in science, commerce, and arts, ensuring that students can pursue their interests and prepare for future academic and career endeavors. The faculty members are experts in their respective fields, committed to mentoring students and guiding them toward achieving their full potential. Beyond academics, St. Francis PU College places a strong emphasis on extracurricular activities. Students are encouraged to participate in sports, cultural events, and various clubs, which help in developing leadership skills, teamwork, and a well-rounded personality. The college regularly organizes seminars, workshops, and guest lectures to provide students with insights into real-world applications of their studies.
